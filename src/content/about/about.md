I'm a Computer Science student at UC Irvine, minoring in Economics and Innovation & Entrepreneurship. I like knowing how things actually work before I build on top of them. That's part of why I built an HTTP server from scratch instead of just reaching for a framework.

I got into AI through research on AI-driven game design at UCI's Games+Learning+Society Center. Since then I've worked through coursework in LLMs, prompt engineering, and agentic AI systems, and used it to build ScoutStat, a football analytics platform with AI-generated scouting reports.

I've also worked on the business side, including an internship in M&A tax technology. Understanding the business behind a product helps me make better calls on the technical side.

Right now I'm looking for opportunities to keep doing both: building AI-powered products and staying close to the business decisions behind them.
